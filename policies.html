Joining Policies

  1. The student makes a reservation in the training course for a portion of the course value, with a minimum of 50% of the training course value, provided that the course value is completed in full after the first lecture or within two months. In the event that the value is not completed, the company has the right to prevent the student from attending.
  2. The trainee has the right to change the booking date or refund the full booking amount before the course start date
  3. Booking is made through the official Facebook page ID @codulefortech or through the company’s official website https://codule.com
  4. The student is not allowed to join the group for the training course with more than one WhatsApp number, and he is not allowed to obtain access to the lectures for the training course with more than one email.
  5. The student has the right to obtain the training course content for life
  6. The study period does not include vacations and holidays
  7. Installments for some training courses are allowed for a maximum of two months
  8. The installments due are paid from the 1st to the 5th of each month, regardless of the booking date or the start date of the course.

Attendance Policies

  1. If the student is late for the start of the lecture by ten minutes, he is considered absent from the lecture
  2. The student has the right to repeat the course or transfer from one group to another without any costs once. Otherwise, an administrative fee will be charged.
  3. The student is allowed to postpone to another training course in the same specialty without any costs once. Otherwise, an administrative fee will be charged.
  4. The student is allowed to transfer from one group to another for the same lecturer for the first time only. Otherwise, an administrative fee will be charged
  5. If the student is more than ten minutes late since the start of the lecture without informing the assistant or the instructor, he is not entitled to enter the lecture.
  6. If the student misses the lecture without a reason, he is entitled to obtain the recording of the lecture, and he is not entitled to attend the lecture again or repeat the lecture again.

Obtain the Training

    The student is not entitled to receive training after the diploma if:
    1. Failure to attend at least 80% of the entire course content
    2. Delivery of 90% of projects
    3. Commitment to submit the tasks required of the lecturer by no less than 80%.
    4. A test is held after the diploma for the student, provided that the trainee’s grade is not less than 80%.

Refund Policies

  1. You must keep the payment receipt, invoice, or transfer receipt because it is not permissible to refund, change the course date, transfer, or postpone without it.
  2. Refunds are permitted within 15 days of attending the first lecture, and no refunds are permitted after that for any reason.
  3. An exceptional refund is allowed for the student in the event that the training course is canceled by the company
  4. In the event of a refund on the same day of participating in the training course, the full amount will be refunded without fees. In the event of a refund after the first two lectures, an administrative fee will be deducted.
  5. The student is not entitled to submit an refund request in the event of his absence from three consecutive lectures or 10 lectures during the duration of the training course.
  6. The recovery period ranges from 10 to 15 working days after sending the complete recovery data
  7. In the event that the student makes a reservation in a full specialization, provided that the full specialization includes more than one specialization, and the student submits a refund request, the specializations that have been completed will be calculated from the full specialization at their original value, with any discounts or facilities in the full specialization being taken away.
  8. Refunds are permitted before the start of the training course without deducting any administrative fees
  9. In the event that the installment is delayed for two days, a warning will be issued first. In the event of a delay of four days from the date of the start of payment of the installment, the course will be stopped and the student is not entitled to recover any amounts or obtain any educational materials from the course.

Obtain the Certificate

Training policies are applied to the policies for obtaining a certificate after a diploma

Job recommendation policies

Only outstanding students are recommended to work in companies affiliated with Codele or with whom they have been contracted or who will be contracted with in the future.
